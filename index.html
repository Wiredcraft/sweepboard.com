Why Octokan?

We love GitHub, but its issue queue, while functional, gets messy pretty quickly:

Visibility
Issues can quickly get buried or become orphan, while attention is focused on the top of the pile.
Prioritization
It is hard to organize what needs to get worked on next or what is more important. The flat structure of the current queue is again focusing too much attention on the latest additions.
Navigation
Participating in threads, capturing new issue while conducting a scrum or simply labeling is hard (lots of clicks/screens).
